Botanic Garden Singapore

Botanic Gardens

Experience

From an ornamental garden with roads, terraces, a band parade area and even a small zoo, it has come a long way in evolving into a leading equatorial botanic garden of 74 hectares, where major world crops, such as rubber and orchids were launched.

What to Look Out!

The Gardens has been developed along a 3-core Concept. The three cores are Tanglin which is the heritage core retaining the old favourites and charms of the historic Gardens; Central is the tourist belt of the Gardens; and Bukit Timah the educational and discovery zone.

How to Get There

Located in central Singapore and just five minutes away from Orchard Road (Singapore's main shopping street).
